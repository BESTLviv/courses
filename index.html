About BEST

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) - is a constantly growing non-profit and non-political organisation. Since 1989 we provide communication, cooperation and exchange possibilities for students all over Europe. 97 Local BEST Groups (LBGs) in 33 countries are creating a growing, well organised, powerful, young and innovative student network for cultural diversity and better understanding of European cultures. Conscious students influence on social development, cooperation and communication. Thus, we manage to bring all the partners in the "student - company - university" triangle closer.

BEST Lviv

LBG Lviv is one of the most productive and biggest LBGs in Ukraine considering the number of its members and organised projects. BEST Lviv was the first founded LBG in Ukraine started its activities in spring 2002 and now develops its activities. In 10 years our organisation has become known and active in Lviv Polytechnic National University. Comparing with previous years, we increased the number of participants at BEST events. All our projects aim to provide students with the opportunity to take active part in university life and organise huge events by themselves. LBG Lviv is a member of BEST. Activities of LBG Lviv are ensured by its Executive Board and several working teams. LBG Lviv Board consists of the President, the Treasurer, the Secretary, and the Vice-President for Human Resources, the Vice-President for Corporate relations, the Vice-President for Information Technology, the Vice-President for Marketing Communications and Public Relations. Together they are responsible for the correct and complete enactment of the decisions of the association.

About Lviv

Do you like festivals? Then you should visit the cultural capital of Ukraine and touristic heart of Eastern Europe. There are hundreds of them. In addition, it is a godsend for coffee-lovers and sweet tooth. There are cozy cafes and chocolates nearly every turn. Do you know what is it? It is Lviv. It is a fantastic city which harmonically combines history and progressive IT development. While drinking a cup of coffee you will always hear other people saying “kolizhanka”(friend), “shpacer”(promenade) and “marynarka”(jacket). In this majestic city first in Ukraine railroad, hotel, based first professional theatre and Ukrainian school invented were invented. Also first in the world cerosine lamp and launched balloon were invented. Primarily Lviv is very typical city but its only at first sight. Besides cozy, narrow streets, ancient temples and different museums people really work here. This is Lviv where special effects for the world famous films like “Hulk” or “Harry Potter” were created. Its one of the most promising developing European cities. Annualy we have more than a million tourists from Poland, Germany, Russia, France, UK, Canada, Japan. Join us!
